## Overview

A design for a baseball clubhouse and surrounding landscape for the Pop Smith Little League, based in New Haven, CT.

## Program

The project combines athlete facilities with a community-facing landscape, providing a welcoming public edge to the field while serving the practical needs of a youth sports program.
