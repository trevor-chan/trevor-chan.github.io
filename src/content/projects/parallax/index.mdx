## Overview

An application of augmented reality to facilitate a more seamless melding of physical and virtual space.

## Approach

Through facial tracking and virtual distortion, perspective shifts resulting from movement of the subject in physical space are translated into corresponding movement and distortion of the camera in virtual space. This provides an illusion of continuity between the physical world and the virtual one displayed on screen.
