## Overview

Design and finite element analysis of an orthopedic femoral bone plate, developed from CT scan geometry to optimize load distribution under physiological loading conditions.

## Simulation

Patient CT scan data was processed to reconstruct femur geometry for use in FEA. Boundary conditions were applied to simulate physiological loading, and iterative design revisions were evaluated for stress distribution and structural integrity.
