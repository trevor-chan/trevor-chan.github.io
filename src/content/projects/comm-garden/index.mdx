## Overview

An urban garden, restaurant, and mixed-use classroom space in downtown New Haven. Hydroponic circulation provides the floating greenhouses with the necessary nutrients to sustain year-round food production.

## Program

The project combines food production, public dining, and community education in a single urban site. Floating greenhouse towers make productive use of vertical space, while ground-level buildings house a community restaurant and flexible classroom facilities.
