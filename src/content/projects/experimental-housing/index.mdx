## Overview

Studies in alternative dwelling structures — a birdhouse, a treehouse, and a series of speculative housing forms exploring the edge between shelter and landscape.

## Program

Each project investigates a different relationship between structure, inhabitation, and site — from the miniature to the human-scale.
