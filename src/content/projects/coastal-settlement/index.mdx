## Overview

An exploration in coastal dwelling: a settlement in which distances and neighbors change with the cycle of the tides. Sea of Cortez, Mexico.

## Program

Walkways change shape according to the water level, becoming passable only during certain times of day. The settlement's social structure and spatial organization are governed by tidal rhythms rather than fixed infrastructure.
