## Overview

Computer vision-enables low-cost neurological physical rehabilitation. Physical rehabilitation is 
essential after a stroke, brain surgery, or brain injury. It requires both teaching rehabilitation 
exercises and regularly assessing impairment to track recovery over time. This would typically 
require weeks to months of sessions with a specialist. A combination of telehealth medicine and 
computer vision offers a low-cost, convenient alternative. 1st place project in 2022 Mission Brain 
Hackathon.
