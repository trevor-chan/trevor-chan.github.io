## Overview

A series of small wood models exploring the relationship between ruled surfaces, joinery, and enclosure. Birch and basswood.

## Program

Each form investigates how a minimal number of planar cuts and bends can produce volumetric complexity. The models function as objects in their own right while also serving as studies for larger structural systems.
